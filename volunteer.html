Volunteer Opportunities

We have various opportunities for you to get involved with our programs and events.

Community Outreach Volunteer

Help us deliver essential goods to local communities in need. Volunteers will assist with sorting and distributing donations.

Time Commitment: 5 hours per week

Event Coordinator Volunteer

Assist in organizing and managing fundraising events. Volunteers will help with event logistics, guest registration, and setup.

Time Commitment: 3-4 hours per event

Online Support Volunteer

Provide remote assistance to our team with administrative tasks, data entry, and communication with supporters.

Time Commitment: Flexible, 2-5 hours per week
